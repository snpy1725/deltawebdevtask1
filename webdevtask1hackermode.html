Welcome to an easy note making resource at your service

You never have to miss another deadline,forget to pick up your wife from work or forget watch your favourite football match!!

First click on the 'create noteboxes' to create 10 textboxes to hold your notes. Adding a 11th note overwrites the 1st and so on

you can add a max of 10 notes at a certain time. To add a note, simply type it in the text box at top and click on submit. for each submit click you will get a popup to tell the color(priority) of the note.Enter this to be either green,red or yellow based on how you would classify the tasks based on priority; Do the same for the next note also.

you can delete and edit the set of notes anytime. just press the required buttons and provide the note number to change or delete.


your priority options are :

  1. green: Least priority
  2. yellow: Medium level priority
  3. red: High level priority
